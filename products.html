CONTROLS

Controls turn off or “turn down” lighting and other equipment when it is not needed, resulting in additional savings. Controls are an important part of energy-efficient lighting solutions. Timers, dusk-to-dawn sensors, occupancy sensors, dimmers, and “daylight harvesting” controls assure that lighting is available when needed, but turned off, or dimmed, when it’s not. Lee Energy can help you select the right controls to reduce lighting energy consumption by 10-40% or more.

REFRIGERATION

Refrigerated cases are a major energy user for many retailers. Since lighting in these cases consumes energy, and adds heat load to the refrigeration system, LEDs make sense, since they give off less heat. Lee Energy offers a variety of energy-saving retrofits for refrigerated cases including:Sloan LED Cold Door reach-in case lighting, DigiLite Open case lighting, Efficient EC evaporator fan motors, Anti-Sweat Heater Controls for reach-in case doors and frames.
